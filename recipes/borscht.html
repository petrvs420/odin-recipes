Description

This borscht recipe comes from my friend's mother from Ukraine who taught me how to make this classic beet soup. It's as authentic as it gets. It can be served vegetarian-style by omitting the sausage.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pork sausage
  • 3 medium beets, peeled and shredded
  • 3 carrots, peeled and shredded
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes, drained

Steps

  1. Crumble sausage into a skillet set over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until no longer pink. Remove from the heat and set aside.
  2. Fill a large pot halfway with water (about 8 cups) and bring to a boil.
  3. Add cabbage and diced tomatoes to pot.
  4. Ladle into serving bowls. Garnish with sour cream and parsley.
